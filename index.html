Bio

I help businesses create value through digital product- starting with a strategic and market POV, taking ideas through discovery, design, buy-in, and implementation.

I love creating teams that really understand what the user and business goals are, and moving in unison.

I've got 15 years of industry experience working with leading startups in New York, London, and San Francisco- often at very early stages, during the chaos phase.

Before digital product lured me in, I worked in alternative asset management building long/short portfolios of consumer, retail, and CPG companies.

Both of my fields are about understanding what people want, how they behave in relation to that, and understanding how things work. This is the personal mindset that runs throughout my work and life. In my personal life, I love things like rock climbing, metal working, and van-fixing...things that demand technical knowledge and opposable thumbs.
